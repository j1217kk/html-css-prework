Journey to Coding Temple

It has been a long journey to get to Coding Temple. I graduated several years ago with a Bachelor's degree in Information Systems. To be completely honest, I chose that major for no real reason other than knowing it was a relevant/safe degree. At the time, I didn't have my priorities as in line as I do now. I landed my first role out of college as an IT Analyst, where I debugged server issues for The U.S. Census Bureau in a windowless, non-team oriented environment. The poor experience really caused me to seek a different career path. I remember that at the time I really didn't have much resolve or passion to begin with for the industry even though it was my major. I ended up becoming an Account Executive over at Yelp in Washington, D.C. selling advertisement slots/perks to businesses. I performed at a high level during my first stint as a salesman and eventually landed a lead development representative role with AWS, where I was involved with the sale of cloud tech. At Amazon, I learned more than I ever have during my days at university. Amazon really changed my professional way of thinking, and they brought the best out of me. I became an avid and active learner; I also began to think of the larger picture in terms of my career. I made a transition over to Technical Recruiting, because I wanted to gain insight into the recruitment process at Amazon. It was a very enjoyable tenure, and I met some of the most impactful people during my year as a Tech Recruiter with Amazon Global Advertising. As a Tech Recruiter, the two main roles I recruited for were Software Development Engineers (back-end focused) as well as Front-End Engineers. After learning and gaining so much insight about the software engineering role/ what it takes to be one at the highest level, as well as talking to engineers every day on the phone... I became inspired. This has led me to today. I wanted to be able to tie all my experience together, and embark on a journey into a new field that I have built both ambition and passion for in software engineering. Coding Temple is the catalyst that will push me towards achieving my goals!
